. Trust-Muldhan: A loan for SME Traders.

Any Bangladeshi businessmen having a Proprietorship firm/Partnership business/ Private Limited Company is successfully operating in urban/rural areas having means and capacity to repay bank loan. This loan is applicable for Small Trading Business and there is no scope of financing for Medium size Trading Enterprise under CMSME.

Features:

  1. Purpose:
    1. To meet up working capital requirement
    2. For expansion of existing business (Repair & Renovation, New Outlet & Building Additional Sstocks, etc.) Cooler, Furniture, Fixture etc. Laptop, Gadgets may be allowed as additional requirements.
  2. Facility Type: Term Loan, OD, Time Loan, L/C, TR.
  3. Loan Tenor: Maximum 48-Months including 6-Month Moratorium period
  4. Loan Amount: BDT 1.00 Lac to BDT. 500.00 Lac.
  5. Interest Rate: As advised by Head Office from time to time.
  6. Security/ Collateral:
    1. Hypothecation of Stock, Receivable, Machineries, etc.
    2. Collateral Free- up to BDT 05.00 Lac.
    3. Personal & Spouse Guarantee
    4. 3rd Party Guarantee.
    5. Post/Undated cheque.
    6. Insurance Coverage (if required)

Eligibility:

  1. Proprietorship/ Partnership/ PVT Ltd.Co.
  2. Minimum 2 (Two)-year of successful business operation of present business.
  3. Age Limit: Minimum - 20 years, Maximum - 60 Years.

 

Required Documents

  1. Valid & Updated Trade License/ Partnership Deed/ Certificate of Incorporation/ MOA
  2. National ID/Smart Card
  3. TIN/VAT Certificate
  4. Bank Statement
  5. Company Seal
  6. Photograph
  7. Updated CIB
  8. Any other Documents (If required).
