1. Trust Nondini (Women Entrepreneurship Loan): Fulfilling the dreams of Women Entrepreneurs

Any Bangladeshi Women Entrepreneur (with minimum skills and specific Succession plan) having minimum 2 (Two) Years of experience for running the business successfully in urban/rural areas and has the capacity to repay the bank loan.

Features:

  1. Purpose:
    1. To meet up working capital requirement
    2. To acquire fixed assets to set up business units/industries or expansion of existing business (Repair & Renovation, New Outlet, Building Additional Stocks etc). Fixed assets that include Plant, Machinery, Air Cooler, Furniture, Fixture etc. Laptop, Gadgets may be allowed as additional requirements.
  2. Facility Type: Term Loan, OD, Time Loan, L/C, TR
  3. Loan Tenor: 5(Five) years including 06 Month grace period.
  4. Loan Amount: BDT 1.00 Lac to 500.00 Lac.
  5. Interest Rate: 5% Interest Rate applicable upon availability of Bangladesh Bank Refinance

                           otherwise advised by Head Office from time to time.

  1. Security/ Collateral:
    1. Hypothecation of Stock, Receivable, Machineries.
    2. Collateral Free Upto BDT 25.00 lac.
    3. Personal & Spouse Guarantee (Guarantee of spouse will be waived in case of Widow or Divorce woman), 3rd Party Guarantee
    4. Post/Undated cheque.
    5. Insurance Coverage (if required)

Eligibility:

  1. Proprietorship firm.
  2. Partnership business/Private Limited Company having 51% or more ownership of women.
  3. Age Limit: Minimum- 20 years, Maximum- 60 Years.

 

Required Documents

  1. Valid & Updated Trade License/ Partnership Deed/ Certificate of Incorporation/ MOA
  2. National ID/Smart Card
  3. TIN/VAT Certificate
  4. Bank Statement
  5. Company Seal
  6. Photograph
  7. Updated CIB
  8. Any other Documents (If required).
