1. Trust Projukti: Loan for Light Engineering

Any proprietorship concern, partnership or private limited company engaged in manufacturing of Light Engineering products with 2(two) years of successful business operation in urban/rural area and who has the means and capacity to repay bank loan is eligible for this loan.

Features:

  1. Purpose: To meet up working capital requirement and to acquire fixed assets to set up business units/industries/ expansion of existing business – repair & renovation, new outlet, building additional stocks.
  2. Facility Type: OD, Time Loan, Term Loan. L/C may be opened to import capital machineries for installation in the same business.
  3. Loan Tenor: Maximum 12 Months for Working Capital facility and Maximum 60 Months for Fixed

                      asset purchase or expansion of business

  1. Loan Amount: BDT 1.00 Lac to BDT. 500.00
  2. Interest Rate: As advised by Head Office from time to time.
  3. Security/ Collateral:
    1. Hypothecation of Stock, Receivable, Machineries, etc.
    2. Collateral Free- up to BDT 05.00 Lac.
    3. Personal & Spouse Guarantee.
    4. 3rd Party Guarantee.
    5. Post/Undated cheque.
    6. Insurance Coverage (if required)

Eligibility:

  1. Proprietorship/ Partnership/ PVT LTD Co.
  2. Minimum 2 (Two)-year of successful business operation of present business.
  3. Age Limit: Minimum - 20 years, Maximum - 60 Years.

 

Required Documents

  1. Valid & Updated Trade License/ Partnership Deed/ Certificate of Incorporation/ MOA.
  2. National ID/Smart Card
  3. TIN/VAT Certificate
  4. Bank Statement
  5. Company Seal
  6. Photograph
  7. Updated CIB
  8. Any other Documents (If required).
