1. Trust Ekota: Group Loan

Small Entrepreneurs Group (for Both Male and Female). Trust Ekota specially designed for group of small entrepreneurs (i.e. Nakshikatha, Tribal tant, pottery, bamboo & cane product, agricultural equipment and other income generating activities) who are deprived of bank loan facility so that they can operate their business smoothly.

Features:

  1. Purpose: To meet up fund requirements for smooth operation and expansion of existing manufacturing business.
  2. Facility Type: Term Loan
  3. Loan Tenor: Minimum 15 months and Maximum 36 months including 6 month moratorium period.
  4. Loan Amount: Minimum – BDT 1.00 Lac, Maximum – BDT 10.00 Lac (for each group)
  5. Interest Rate: For Woman Group: 5% Interest Rate applicable upon availability of Bangladesh Bank

                         Refinance otherwise advised by Head Office from time to time.

  1. Security/ Collateral:
    1. Hypothecation of Stock, Receivable, Machineries, etc.
    2. Collateral Free- up to BDT 05.00 Lac
    3. Personal & Group Guarantee.
    4. Post/Undated cheque.
    5. Insurance Coverage (if required)

Eligibility:

  1. Proprietorship Farm.
  2. Minimum 2 (Two)-year of successful business operation of present business.
  3. Age Limit: Minimum - 20 years, Maximum - 60 Years.

 

Required Documents

  1. Valid & Updated Trade License. Flexibility of Trade License for Loan limit upto BDT 5.00 Lac (In case of group loan).
  2. National ID/Smart Card
  3. TIN/VAT Certificate
  4. Bank Statement
  5. Company Seal
  6. Photograph
  7. Updated CIB
  8. Any other Documents (If required).
