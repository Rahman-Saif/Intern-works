Brig. Gen. Md. Sajjad Hossain

SUP, NDC, AFWC, PSC

Brig Gen Sajjad of the Bangladesh Army, born on 22 November 1972 in Rangamati, was commissioned on 16 December 1993. He holds three master's degrees in national security, strategy and development, and defense studies from prestigious institutions, along with diplomas and certifications in supply chain, operations, HR, project management, and strategic leadership. His diverse career includes commanding regimental units, serving as an instructor at ASC Centre and School and Bangladesh Military Academy, and holding staff roles such as ADC to CAS and Director of Welfare and Rehabilitations. He has also contributed significantly in three UN missions in logistics and training roles.

Mr. Anisuddin Ahmed Khan

OSP, AFWC, PSC

Mr. Anisuddin Ahmed Khan, a Fellow of the Institute of Bankers, Bangladesh, is a seasoned banker and former Managing Director & CEO of Mutual Trust Bank Limited (2009-2019). With over 38 years of experience across leading financial institutions, he specializes in corporate banking, credit operations, risk management, and financial inclusion. He has presented papers at Harvard and Yale, attended leadership training at Cambridge and INSEAD, and served as Chairman of ABB and PDBL. Currently, he is an Adjunct Professor at IUB, Independent Director at Berger Paints, and involved in multiple corporate and philanthropic organizations.

Brig. Gen. Md. Mohashin Reza

OSP, AFWC, PSC

Brigadier General Md Mohashin Reza, OSP, afwc, psc, commissioned in 1997, has commanded various units in the Bangladesh Army and served in key staff and instructional roles, including at DSCSC, Dhaka. A graduate of DSCSC Bangladesh and the Royal Saudi Arabian Combined Staff College, he also completed the Armed Forces War Course at NDC, Bangladesh, earning a master's in defense studies. He participated in three UN missions, commanding a multinational task force in MINUSMA, Mali, and was awarded the Outstanding Service Medal for a successful operation. He enjoys music and reading.

Ahsan Zaman Chowdhury

DMD & CEO

Mr. Ahsan Zaman Chowdhury, a seasoned banking professional with nearly four decades of experience, has held key roles at AB Bank, Eastern Bank Limited, and Trust Bank PLC. Joining Trust Bank in 2016, he rose to Deputy Managing Director and Chief Business Officer in 2020 and later served as Managing Director (In-Charge). Renowned for his expertise in business development, corporate banking, and strategic decision-making, he has driven profitability and excellence at Trust Bank. A graduate of the University of Chittagong, he is also an avid golfer, traveler, and active member of socio-cultural organizations, embodying leadership and dedication in all facets of his life.
