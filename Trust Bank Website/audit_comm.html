Audit Committee Chairman

Nusrat Khan

Nusrat Khan, born on 13 March 1983 in Dhaka, is a charismatic and hardworking leader who inspires peers with her dedication and excellence. She began her career as a lecturer at East West University and later joined the Department of Finance at the University of Dhaka, where she is now an Associate Professor. With an MSc in Management of Risks (distinction) from St. John's University, New York, she is a Fulbright Scholar and a member of the Beta Gamma Sigma honor society. Renowned for her academic excellence and numerous journal publications, she also serves as an independent director of Bangladesh Welding Electrodes Ltd. Her achievements have earned her widespread admiration in academia and beyond.

Audit Committee Member

Brig. Gen. Selim Azad

HDMC, PSC

Brigadier General Selim Azad, hdmc, psc was born on 22 March 1976 in a reputed Muslim family in Dhaka. He was commissioned with 34 BMA Long Course on 07 June 1996 in the corps of Infantry. In a span of 28 years with Bangladesh Army, the officer has held a variety of important command, instructional and staff appointments. In his personal life, Brigadier General Selim Azad is happily married and blessed with two sons.
