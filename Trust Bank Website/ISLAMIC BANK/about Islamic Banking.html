Islam is a comprehensive way of life (deen), addressing both material and spiritual dimensions. Allah (SWT) emphasizes its completeness in the Quran:

“This day I have perfected your religion for you, completed My blessing on you, and approved Islam as the way of life for you” (Al-Maidah: 3).

Islamic banking aligns with Shariah, prohibiting interest (riba) and ensuring business activities are halal. It offers both worldly profit and divine reward by adhering to Allah's commands. To ensure Shariah compliance, Islamic banking maintains robust documentation, eliminating vagueness in business dealings and responsibilities.

Shariah Supervisory Committee

The Shari'ah Supervisory Committee of the Bank plays a vital role in framing and enforcing policy for strict adherence to Shari'ah principles in the Bank. As per the Islamic Banking Guidelines circulated by Bangladesh Bank, the Board of Directors forms an independent Shariah Supervisory Committee (SSC) with experienced individuals in Islamic Jurisprudence. Trust Bank's Shari'ah Supervisory Committee is composed of 3 prominent Ulama knowledgeable in Fiqhul Moamalat, providing guidance on the Bank’s Shari'ah compliance.
